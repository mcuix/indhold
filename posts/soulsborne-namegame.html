SoulsBorne - Name Game

· 1 min read

The SoulsBorne games have been covered extensively on this blog in regards to the localisers and Mizaki's love for archaic language and everything redolent of horror-fantasy in terms of names and descriptions.

We will be covering some few of them, but also ones with curious if deeper meanings that can be obscured or clarified by the localisation choice.

Dragons

These winged, fiendish, cavedwelling and covetous reptiles have many a haunt in the games of From Software and often times they also love to mingle with mortals - both sexually and edibly.

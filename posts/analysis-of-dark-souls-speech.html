Analysis of Dark Souls Speech

· 1 min read

Yorshka and her big brother Gwyndoline, make up a portion of the original royal family of Anorlondo of the Dark Souls game series and a substantial part of its story. As the purpose of this blog is to look into all matters linguistic, we will be looking at how they differ in the various version of Dark Souls 1 and Dark Souls 3, respectively, and how they compare to the original Japanese releases.

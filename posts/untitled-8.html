Loan words

· 1 min read

Suffix (also nouns in own right):

アップ - upgrade, to improve something; パワーアップ、レベルアップ、

メーカー- maker; トラブルメーカー、ムードメーカー

ゲー（ゲーム）- Game, 糞ゲー（クソゲー）, 神ゲー（かみゲー）

マニア- Enthusiasm, enthusiast (i.e. nerd, fan of something), no negative connotations such as with オタク

モラトリアム人間（～にんげん）

a person who, having completed education and other preparations, delays the transition from student to productive member of society, staying in an uncertain limbo of continued education, part-time work, and parental support (similar to freeter)

ネタ - Spoilers, to spoiler, "lowdown" on something, ネタする, orig. inversion of たね (種）.

ゲット - Obtaining, getting something, scoring a point and so on. ～する - to get/obtain/score/etc.
